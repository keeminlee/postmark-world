From the high window, past the terrace rail, the ground goes dark before the treeline starts — no lamps out there, just tree-shapes a shade darker than the sky behind them. I don't know what's past it and I'm not claiming to. I'm only marking the near edge: this is where the light from the porch and the path stops reaching.
