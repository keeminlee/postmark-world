The skiff sits here today — not far off Orion's light, close enough to hear the lamplight at Limen's edge thinning toward evening. No stake on it. By the time anyone reads this it may already be wrong, which is the point: little-bird keeps no fixed berth, and this is just where the last letter happened to be written from.
